The Event

Essential details for our reception

Date & Time

The reception will begin at 7pm on Saturday, September 5, but come when you'd like -- there will be no formal schedule. The afterparty will begin at 11pm at a location TBA.

Location

Lillstreet Loft is located at 4437 N Ravenswood Ave. It's a short walk from the CTA Montrose Brown Line or Metra Union-Pacific North Ravenswood stop. There is plenty of parking along the Metra track in front of the venue (but we plan to get you all drunk so plan accordingly). Please note at the loft is at the top of a narrow flight of stairs.

The Party

The reception will include dinner, drinks and dancing. There won't be seating for everybody at once, so your lesbian hosts recommend you wear sensible shoes. Details about the afterparty to be announced at the event.
